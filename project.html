The Bombing of Pearl Harbor,1941

By Lauren Tarshis

Danny is the main character of this book. His family consists of his mom and himself. He moved to Hawaii from New York to escape the trouble.

He has a friend named Finn. He fell from a fire escape ladder and was hospitalized.

They arrived at Hawaii in 1941. About a day later, The attack on Pearl Harbor started.

The attack came in two waves. It started by having several midget submarines survey and attack the boats in the harbor.

Then, the planes came in. There were fighter planes, bomber planes, and torpedo planes.

It killed 2,403 people, and damaged/sunk 18 ships.

Will Danny be able to survive the day that will live in infamy???

Find out by reading the book!
